Background and interests

I study Artificial Intelligence, with a strong interest in computer vision, deep learning, robotics and intelligent systems applied to visual data.

I have a background in Computer Science and I am currently pursuing an MSc in Artificial Intelligence. Over time, I have become especially interested in projects where AI interacts with images, sensors, real environments and systems that need to make robust decisions.

My main interests include computer vision, deep learning, multimodal learning and robotics. I enjoy working on complete pipelines: from data handling and experimentation to model evaluation and integration into real-time systems or working prototypes.

I have worked on projects involving medical imaging, perception for autonomous systems, robotic teleoperation and computational optimisation. I try to keep a practical but rigorous approach, with reproducible experiments, error analysis, model comparison and attention to data quality.
